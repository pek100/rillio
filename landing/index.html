Rillio brings movies, series and live TV together through open add-ons and plays them in a native 4K player, while keeping your library on your device, encrypted and yours.

Download for Windows View on GitHub

No account required. Windows today, macOS and Linux coming.

One app for all of it

Discover, organize and watch from any source, held together by an open add-on ecosystem.

Everything in one place

Add-ons pull catalogs, subtitles and streams from across the web into a single, tidy library. You choose what to plug in.

Native 4K playback

An in-app player handles 4K HDR and Dolby Vision natively. Smooth, sharp, and free of browser codec limits.

Pick up anywhere

Your library and watch progress stay in sync across your devices, optionally, and end-to-end encrypted when they do.

Private by default

Most apps treat privacy as a setting. Rillio treats it as the default, the quiet stream the name is named for.

Your data stays yours

No account needed. Your library and history live on your device by default, not on someone else's server.

Encrypted DNS

Every name lookup goes out over DNS-over-HTTPS, encrypted, not broadcast in plaintext to whoever is listening.

Quiet networking

The streaming engine stays a quiet client by default and never advertises you as a seeder, and you can route it through your own VPN or proxy.

Auditable, not a black box

The engine is open-source Rust you can read and verify. The same code, running locally, with nothing hidden.

Signed updates

Every update is cryptographically signed and verified before it installs, so you always get the real thing.

No ads, no tracking

No trackers, no telemetry by default, no ads between you and what you came to watch.

Start watching, quietly.
